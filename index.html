Account Deletion – DreamLogger

DreamLogger respects users' privacy and allows account and personal data deletion at any time.

How to request account deletion

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please send an email to:

support@dreamlogger.app
Subject: Account Deletion – DreamLogger

In the email, please include:

  • The email address used to create the account
  • A clear request to delete your account

What happens after the request

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • All dream records and associated personal data will be removed
  • The deletion process will be completed within 30 days

Data that may be retained

No personal data is retained after account deletion, except where required by law.
